About Us

Iya Bolu Food Store specializes in sourcing, packaging, and shipping authentic African food products worldwide. Whether it’s garri, egusi, ogbono, palm oil, dried fish, or other staples, we make sure you get them fresh and at the same price they are sold here in Nigeria. Our goal is to bridge the gap between you and home, giving you the comfort of enjoying the real taste of Africa no matter where you live. With reliable packaging, trusted logistics partners, and a commitment to quality, Iya Bolu Food Store is your dependable link to the foods you love and miss.
